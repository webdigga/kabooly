How The CRM Works

1

Choose Your Plan

Pick the plan that matches your business size. Start with Starter for smaller teams, or go straight to Professional or Enterprise if you need more capacity.

2

Set Up Your Account

Create your account and invite your team. Set roles and permissions so everyone has the right level of access. Import your existing contacts from spreadsheets or other systems.

3

Start Managing Contacts

Add contacts, track interactions, create deals, and send your first campaigns. The interface is straightforward, so your team can start using it right away.

4

Grow With Confidence

As your business grows, upgrade your plan to get more contacts, team members, and email capacity. Your data stays safe and secure throughout.
